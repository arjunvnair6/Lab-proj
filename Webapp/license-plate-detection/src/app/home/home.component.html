Abstarct

Every country now has a severe issue with traffic regulation and vehicle ownership identification. It might be challenging to recognize car owners who drive excessively fast and against road regulations. Due to this, this becomes difficult to capture and punish those individuals since traffic officials may not be able to obtain the vehicle's license plate from a moving vehicle due to its speed. As one of the answers to this problem, it is necessary to design an automatic number plate recognition system. One smart transportation and detecting method is the number plate recognition system. This mix of technologies allows the program to automatically recognize and read the license plate number of a vehicle from digitally taken photographs. Identifying and converting a digital image's pixel data into the number plate's plain text data is known as automatically taking a license plate. The primary goal is to employ and combine several morphological operations in a way that makes it possible to recognize and correctly interpret the license plate of a specific car. This is based on several operations, including image enhancement, edge detection using tensor flow, and extracting the license plate number from a vehicle's photo using easy OCR. Following the completion of the previous phases, the segmentation method is now used to locate the text on the number plate by matching the template and OCR. This method can rapidly and effectively identify the license plate number from an image of the car. With Angular as the front end and Flask as the back end, the entire application is set up as a web application and backend connected to machine learning.
